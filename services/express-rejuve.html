How It Works

The Express Rejuve is designed for people who need real therapeutic results but do not have an hour to spare. In just 30 minutes, focused hydromassage jets target the one or two areas causing you the most discomfort — whether that is a locked-up neck from hours at a screen, a stiff lower back, or tension headaches that will not quit.

Because the session is concentrated on fewer zones, the water jets can work at higher intensity for longer on each area, often delivering more targeted relief than a longer full-body session. Many of our clients book Express Rejuve on their lunch breaks and return to work feeling like a different person.
