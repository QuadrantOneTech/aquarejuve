How It Works

This treatment combines two powerful modalities into a single luxurious session. A mineral-rich wrap — blended with sea clay, algae extracts, and essential oils — is applied to your skin, creating a gentle thermal cocoon that opens pores and draws impurities to the surface. While the wrap works, warm hydromassage jets underneath stimulate circulation and lymphatic flow, amplifying the detoxification process.

The result is a treatment that works from the outside in and the inside out simultaneously. Your skin absorbs nourishing minerals while releasing what it no longer needs. Most clients notice visibly smoother, more radiant skin immediately after their first session.

What to Expect

1

Dry Brushing Prep

A full-body dry brush exfoliation removes dead skin cells and stimulates circulation, preparing your skin to absorb the maximum benefit from the mineral wrap.

2

Mineral Wrap + Hydromassage

The warm botanical wrap is applied head to toe, then you settle onto the hydromassage bed. Gentle water jets work beneath you as the wrap draws out toxins and infuses your skin with minerals.

3

Rinse & Moisturize

A warm water rinse washes away the wrap, followed by a rich botanical moisturizer that seals in hydration and leaves your skin silky and luminous.
