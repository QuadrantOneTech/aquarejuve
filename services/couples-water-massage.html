Couples Water Massage

Unwind together in our private couples suite with side-by-side hydromassage beds, soft aromatherapy, and warm ambient lighting — the perfect way to reconnect and recharge as a pair.

60 / 90 min From $179 / couple

How It Works

Our couples suite features two full hydromassage beds positioned side by side in a beautifully appointed private room. Each bed is independently customizable, so both partners receive a treatment tailored to their own needs — whether one prefers gentle relaxation and the other wants deep pressure work.

The room is infused with your choice of essential oil blends, bathed in warm candlelight tones, and designed so you can enjoy the experience together from start to finish. It is wellness as a shared moment, not just a treatment.

What to Expect

1

Consultation Together

Your specialist meets with both of you to understand preferences — pressure, temperature, focus areas — and selects an aromatherapy blend you both enjoy.

2

Synchronized Side-by-Side Session

Settle into your private suite as both beds begin in unison. Each treatment is individually calibrated, but timed together so you share every moment of the experience.

3

Shared Relaxation Lounge

After your session, enjoy quiet time together in the suite with herbal tea, fruit, and — for Premium guests — champagne and artisan chocolates.
