How It Works

Our recovery therapy uses high-pressure, precision-targeted water jets that penetrate deep into fatigued muscle tissue. The intense hydrodynamic force flushes lactic acid, reduces inflammation, and increases blood flow to damaged fibers — all without the bruising or soreness that can follow aggressive manual sports massage.

Water temperature alternates between warm and cool phases to create a contrast therapy effect, stimulating your circulatory system and speeding up the body's natural repair processes. Whether you train daily or are coming back from an injury, this treatment meets your body where it is.

What to Expect

1

Movement Assessment

Your specialist evaluates your range of motion, identifies areas of tightness or injury, and builds a targeted recovery protocol around your training demands.

2

High-Pressure Targeted Recovery

Powerful water jets work through each muscle group with alternating warm and cool streams, flushing metabolic waste and driving fresh, oxygenated blood into damaged tissue.

3

Cool-Down Stretch

A gentle cool-water phase calms inflammation while your specialist guides you through assisted stretching to lock in your restored range of motion.
