Pressure Hydromassage

Targeted water jets at variable pressures work deep into muscle tissue, releasing tension without the discomfort of traditional elbow-and-thumb techniques.

45 / 60 / 90 min From $59

How It Works

Our pressure hydromassage system delivers precisely calibrated water jets through a specialized bed or chamber. You lie comfortably while streams of warm, pressurized water target specific muscle groups. The pressure is fully adjustable — from a gentle 1 PSI for relaxation to an intense 10 PSI for deep tissue work — and can be varied across different body zones within a single session.

Unlike manual massage where pressure depends on the therapist's strength and technique, our water jets deliver perfectly consistent force that never fatigues, never varies, and penetrates up to 3x deeper than human hands.

What to Expect

1

Consultation

Your specialist maps your tension points and tailors the pressure zones, temperature, and jet patterns to your body's needs.

2

The Session

Warm pressurized water jets systematically work through each muscle group — neck, shoulders, back, glutes, legs. You control the intensity at any time.

3

Cool Down

A gentle tapering phase with reduced pressure and aromatherapy eases you back. You'll leave feeling deeply relaxed yet energized.

  • Desk workers with postural strain
  • General stress and anxiety relief
  • Anyone who finds traditional deep tissue too painful
  • Regular maintenance and preventive care
